    OP Consultancy

    Comprehensive outpatient services for patients seeking expert consultations across various specialties.

    Our OP Consultancy provides accessible and expert medical care with short waiting times. Patients can consult experienced doctors for diagnosis, follow-up, and treatment planning—all in a comfortable, patient-first environment.

    Emergency Consultancy

    Round-the-clock emergency care handled by trained medical professionals with speed and compassion.

    Our emergency unit is equipped to handle all types of urgent medical conditions—from minor injuries to life-threatening situations. Immediate diagnosis, stabilization, and referrals ensure patients receive the right care when it matters most.

    Pharmacy

    Reliable in-house pharmacy providing genuine medications and timely service.

    Our hospital pharmacy ensures patients get prescribed medicines without hassle. We stock a wide range of trusted brands and provide pharmacist guidance for safe usage. Open daily to serve both inpatients and outpatients.

    Laboratory

    Accurate, fast, and reliable diagnostic testing to support medical decisions.

    Our diagnostic lab offers advanced testing including blood, urine, and pathology reports. With experienced technicians and automated systems, we ensure high accuracy and quick results that help in timely treatment planning.

    Radiology

    Advanced imaging services to assist in early diagnosis and treatment planning.

    Our radiology department is equipped with modern X-ray, ultrasound, and imaging facilities. Radiologists work closely with doctors to provide detailed insights that guide patient care. We prioritize safety, speed, and clarity in all scans.
